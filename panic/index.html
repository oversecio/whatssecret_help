Panic

Activating Panic-Mode will immediately

  • forget all entered passwords for "simple" encryption
  • close all unlocked symmetric keys
  • close all cached keys in OpenKeyChain
  • remove all WhatsSecret overlays, buttons and notifications.
  • force-stop WhatsSecret

so that no indication of anything "secret" will be left on the device (Of course an investigator will still see that the WhatsSecret app is installed on the device)

Panic-Mode can be activated by

  • Long-tapping the Hide Button
  • Tapping "STOP" in the WhatsSecret notification

To re-enable WhatsSecret after Panic-Mode has been entered, go to the main WhatsSecret app and tap on "Reactivate"
